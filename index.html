Bmi calculator

Body mass index (BMI) is a medical screening tool that estimates the amount of body fat based on the ratio of height to weight. It is calculated by dividing weight in kilograms by the square of height in meters. BMI can help determine whether a person is at an unhealthy or healthy weight and may be used to estimate the risk of certain diseases.

The formula is Kg*M^2
